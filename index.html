About TaleemPK

Pakistan's first comprehensive university comparison platform — helping students make informed decisions about fees, merit, and programs, all in one place.

Our Mission

Every Pakistani Student Deserves to Make the Right Decision

Every year, hundreds of thousands of students in Pakistan navigate the university admission process without access to reliable, consolidated information — fees, merit, programs, and deadlines scattered across dozens of sources.

TaleemPK bridges that gap. We bring together data on 218 HEC-recognized universities in one place — completely free, for every student, everywhere in Pakistan.

Our goal is simple: No more guessing. No more confusion.
